Our TOP's

Icebreaker by Hannah Grace

Description: A contemporary romance about Ana Allen, a college student who unexpectedly bonds with Nathan Hawkins, a talented hockey player. As their relationship grows, they navigate personal struggles, athletic pressures, and undeniable chemistry.

Review: I personally loved it! If you are looking for a book that is a quick, easy read with sports romance, then Icebreaker is great!

A Court of Silver Flames by Sarah J. Maas

Description: Nesta Archeron has always been prickly-proud, swift to anger, and slow to forgive. And ever since being forced into the Cauldron and becoming High Fae against her will, she's struggled to find a place for herself within the strange, deadly world she inhabits.

Review: Wow! Just absolutely my favorite book after ACOWAR! the characters development in this story was SURRIEL

The Cruel Prince by Holly Black

Description: Jude was seven years old when her parents were murdered and she and her two sisters were stolen away to live in the treacherous High Court of Faerie. Ten years later, Jude wants to belong there, despite her mortality. But many of the fey despise humans. Especially Prince Cardan, the youngest and wickedest son of the High King.

Review: i loved this book, i have just started to get into book but i loved this one so much and i couldn't stop reading. I finished it in like two days.

They Both Die at the End By Adam Silvera

Description: Mateo and Rufus are total strangers, but, for different reasons, they’re both looking to make a new friend on their End Day.

Review:This book broke me and then put the pieces back together all on its own. It taught me love and how every moment should be lived to the fullest because we never know which can be the last.

Twisted Love by Ana Huang

Description: Alex Volkov is a devil blessed with the face of an angel and cursed with a past he can't escape. But when he's forced to look after his best friend's sister, he starts to feel something in his chest: A crack. A melt.

Review: the other books in the series are WAY better but that doesn’t make this one bad, it’s good enough but didn’t exactly do it for me like twisted hate/games did

The Fine Print by Lauren Asher

Description: Rowan I'm in the business of creating fairy tales. Theme parks. Production companies. Five-star hotels. Everything could be all mine if I renovated Dreamland. My initial idea of hiring Zahra was good in theory, but then I kissed her.

Review: I really didn’t think I would like this, but I’m mad at the second book is not out. Take from that what you will.

Pride and Prejudice by Jane Austen

Description: . A novel of manners, it follows the character development of Elizabeth Bennet, the protagonist of the book, who learns about the repercussions of hasty judgments and comes to appreciate the difference between superficial goodness and actual goodness.

Review:I do not know how many times I have read this book but each time is as if its for the first time! Love everything about it, a true classic.

A Good's Girl Guide To Murder by Holly Jackson

Description: . The case is closed. Five years ago, schoolgirl Andie Bell was murdered by Sal Singh. The police know he did it. Everyone in town knows he did it.But when Pip starts uncovering secrets that someone in town desperately wants to stay hidden

Review:10/10. One of the best thrillers ever. The complete book is filled with twists.

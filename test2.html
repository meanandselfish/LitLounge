Why you should start reading?

Reading is a habit that can improve your lifespan by reducing your stress, enhancing your mental health, developing new neural pathways, helping you learn empathy, improving your ability to communicate, and providing you with healthy entertainment. There may be a lot of new hobbies in the digital world, but reading is one of the few habits that improve your lifespan. In fifteen to thirty minutes a day, you can enjoy all of these amazing brain benefits and set you on the path to unlock your limitless life.

Where you should start?

To find the kind of books that can help you forget the rest of the world, you’ll have to build the habit of active reading. The best books to read for beginners are quick, simple English novels, entertaining easy reads with small-medium sized chapters. Not all of us can pick up a highly recommended book and enjoy it as intended. If you get distracted easily, or find it difficult to sit down long enough to finish a book, that’s pretty normal. Don’t be discouraged if you didn’t breeze through that 800 page novel recommended by the internet or a friend. The best books to read for beginners are often overlooked as recommendations.
